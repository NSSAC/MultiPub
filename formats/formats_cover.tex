Description:& STDN‑GEN is a framework that uses large language models to construct four‑level "shallow" technology dependency networks, linking selected technologies to their components, raw materials, and main producing countries. The report describes the data sources, model orchestration, and validation steps used to generate over 150 such networks across six technology areas, and presents two case studies (gallium in clean‑energy technologies and rare‑earth supply chains) to illustrate how the approach can inform supply‑chain risk assessment and policy analysis.
